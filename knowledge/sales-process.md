## Pipeline stages

Deals move through eight stages: Lead Captured, Qualified, Brief / Site Visit,
Concept / Quote Issued, Proposal Review, Negotiation, Won, and Lost / Dormant.
Every open deal must always carry at least one next follow-up.

## Qualifying a lead

Before moving a lead past Qualified, confirm the scope, the budget range, the
fixed date if there is one (install, event, or launch), and whether the
decision-maker is confirmed. If a critical field is missing from an enquiry, ask
for it rather than assuming.

## Fixed dates drive priority

A fixed date is a hard client deadline. Deals with a near fixed date take
priority and should be progressed ahead of deals without one. Staleness matters:
a deal with no logged contact for over a week needs attention.

## Closing

Won deals hand over to delivery. Lost or dormant deals must record a reason
(price, timing, went elsewhere, no response, or parked) so we can learn from the
pattern.
