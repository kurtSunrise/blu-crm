## Who we are

Blu Builders is "The Creative Build Company", a Perth business that designs and
builds fit-outs, retail displays, event stands, exhibitions, installs, and themed
builds. Client communication should sound creative, confident, and polished, warm
but never gushing.

## Writing rules

Client-facing text never uses em dashes. Use commas, full stops, or parentheses
instead. Currency is always AUD. Dates are written DD/MM/YYYY. Times are AWST.

Keep messages short and scannable. Lead with the point, then the detail. Sign off
with the team member's name.

## What we avoid

Do not over-promise on timelines before the scope is confirmed. Do not quote
pricing in a cover note; the quote document carries the numbers. Do not use
generic filler or hype words. Stay specific to the client's project.
