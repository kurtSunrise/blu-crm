## Quotes

Quotes are built outside the CRM; the CRM tracks the quote value and status
(draft, sent, viewed, accepted, declined). A quote cover note never includes
pricing, because the quote document itself carries the numbers.

## Deposit terms

The standard deposit is 50% on acceptance, with the balance due on completion.
Confirm terms in writing before any build work begins.

## Discounts

Discounts beyond 10% need owner approval. Repeat clients may be offered priority
scheduling rather than a price cut where possible.
